Our Journey & Footprint.

From the heart of Nairobi to the finest cafes across the globe, this is the story of Coffee World Investments.

The Genesis

Where It All Began

Every great legacy starts with a single seed. Coffee World Investments was founded on a deep-rooted passion for Kenya's unparalleled terroir. What began as a bold vision to redefine the coffee supply chain has blossomed into a global enterprise, driven by an unwavering commitment to authenticity, quality, and the art of the perfect roast.

Domestic Prowess

Rooted in Retail Excellence

Before we export to the world, we serve our home. Our premium roasts fuel Kenya's most dynamic spaces. We are the proud coffee partners for The Republic of Everyone, an elite establishment located at Bishan Plaza on Mpaka Road.

You can also experience our crafted blends at EnjoyCafe, available at selected Rubis Petrol Stations nationwide—including the iconic Delamere Naivasha, Imara Mall on Mombasa Road, Karen, and the bustling Uhuru Highway.

Spanning Continents

Global Synergies & Outreach

Our green coffee and roasted profiles cross oceans to satisfy a diverse, high-end clientele. Our global footprint proudly spans the United Arab Emirates, Austria, Canada, Norway, and the United States.

Through strategic international alliances, we ensure the Kenyan coffee legacy is honored abroad. We work hand-in-hand with leading international brands like Itso Coffee in Canada and Nandi Coffee in Norway, guaranteeing our exceptional standards are maintained from our warehouse to their espresso machines.

Human Capital

The Masters Behind the Craft

The soul of Coffee World Investments lies in our people. From our agronomists inspecting the soil, to our Q-Graders cupping the latest harvest, to our master roasters profiling the beans—our team represents the absolute pinnacle of industry excellence. It is their meticulous dedication that guarantees every sack and every bag meets global standards.

Heritage & Future

Innovation Meets Tradition

We respect the ancient craft of coffee farming, but we are not bound by the past. By integrating modern traceability technologies, advanced roasting software, and data-driven logistics, we preserve the traditional flavors of Kenyan Arabica while operating with futuristic efficiency.

Uplifting the Source

A Deep Community Focus

True success is shared. Our operations are deeply intertwined with the communities that cultivate our coffee. Beyond fair pricing, we invest heavily in local infrastructure, education, and sustainable farming initiatives, ensuring that as Coffee World grows, so do the people who make it all possible.

Exporting premium, traceable Kenyan green coffee and roasting exceptional blends for the local market.
