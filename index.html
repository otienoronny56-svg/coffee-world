The Kenyan Coffee Legacy

Coffee World Investments is more than just an exporter. We are a bridge between the high-altitude volcanic soils of Kenya and the world's most discerning roasters.

Every bean we produce reflects decades of knowledge, care, and tradition. From our partner farms to your cup, we are committed to delivering coffee that tells a story of resilience, heritage, and exceptional quality.

Iconic Flavours

Dark Roast

Bold and smoky with notes of dark chocolate and rich caramel. Our Dark Roast offers a full-bodied experience with low acidity, perfect for a powerful and intense cup.

Medium Roast

Smooth and perfectly balanced. Discover harmonious notes of toasted nuts and milk chocolate with a bright, clean finish. The quintessential all-day coffee.

Freshly Ground

Convenience meets quality. Our signature blends, ground to perfection for your preferred brewing method, unlocking a consistently fresh and aromatic cup every time.

Gourmet Blends

An exploration of the extraordinary. These curated single-origin and micro-lot coffees feature complex, wine-like acidity with vibrant notes of fruit and florals.
