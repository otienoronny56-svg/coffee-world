From Seed to Cup

The meticulous journey of Coffee World Investments' premium Kenyan coffee.

Step 1

Planting the Seed

Our journey begins in the high-altitude volcanic soils of Mount Kenya. We partner with select cooperatives in Nyeri and Kirinyaga, where our Arabica seedlings are nurtured under the shade of native trees, developing the deep, complex flavors that define Kenyan coffee.

Step 2

Selective Harvesting

At Coffee World Investments, quality is paramount. Our partner farmers meticulously hand-pick only the ripest, deep-red coffee cherries. This selective harvesting ensures that only the sweetest and most mature beans make it to the next stage, guaranteeing a consistently superior cup.

Step 3

Sun-Kissed Drying

The harvested cherries are processed and laid out on raised African drying beds. Under the watchful eye of the Kenyan sun, the beans are turned regularly by hand. This slow, natural drying process, managed at our partner washing stations, is crucial for developing the coffee's bright acidity and heavy body.

Step 4

Milling & Grading

Once dried, the parchment coffee is transported to our central mill in Nairobi. Here, it's hulled, polished, and graded by size, density, and quality. Our state-of-the-art equipment and expert Q-graders ensure that only the best beans, like the coveted AA and PB grades, are selected for the CWI brand.

Step 5

The Art of Roasting

For our local retail market, our master roasters take over. In our Nairobi facility, each batch is roasted to perfection, unlocking its unique flavor profile. Whether it's a bold Dark Roast or a balanced Medium, our roasting is a science and an art, tailored to highlight the bean's origin.

Step 6

Packaging & Preservation

Freshness is key. Our roasted beans are immediately packed in high-quality, one-way valve bags that preserve their aromatic compounds. Our branding reflects the premium quality within, ready for our retail partners and direct customers.

Step 7

Marketing & Distribution

Our story is shared through our presence in Kenya's finest cafes, like The Republic of Everyone, and through our participation in global trade shows. We connect directly with roasters and buyers, showcasing the traceability and superior quality that CWI represents.

Step 8

Export & Shipping

For our international clients, the final step is logistics. Our green coffee is expertly packed into GrainPro bags and shipped from Mombasa to ports across the world. We manage the entire export process, ensuring our premium Kenyan coffee arrives safely and on time, ready for roasting.

Exporting premium, traceable Kenyan green coffee and roasting exceptional blends for the local market.
